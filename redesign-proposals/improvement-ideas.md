\# Improvement Ideas



\## Idea 01 – Improve product page information hierarchy

The product detail page should better prioritize essential information such as title, price, top features, and key benefits. Long text blocks and dense specification areas could be simplified or grouped into collapsible sections to reduce overload and improve scanability.



\## Idea 02 – Simplify product title presentation

The product page could improve scanability by visually separating the product name from secondary attributes. Key details such as compatibility, waterproof level, or special features could be displayed as tags or bullet highlights instead of being packed into the title.



\## Idea 03 – Make key benefits easier to scan

The “About this item” section could be improved by shortening bullet points, highlighting top benefits, and using clearer formatting for fast scanning. Important selling points should stand out visually in the first seconds of product review.
