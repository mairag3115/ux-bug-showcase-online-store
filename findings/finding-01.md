\# Finding 01



\## Title

Product page presents excessive information density



\## Type

UX Finding



\## Severity

Medium



\## Priority

High



\## Screen Analyzed

Amazon product detail page



\## Observation

The product detail page contains a very large amount of information competing for the user's attention at the same time. The title is extremely long, the specifications section is dense, and the “About this item” section includes large text blocks that make scanning difficult.



\## Why It Matters

Users should be able to quickly identify the most important purchase information such as product name, price, key features, and main benefits. When too much content is presented without stronger hierarchy, the page feels overwhelming and slows down decision-making.



\## User Impact

\- Harder to scan key information quickly

\- Increased cognitive load

\- More effort required to understand the product

\- Possible frustration during the shopping journey



\## Evidence

Use screenshot evidence from the Amazon product page showing the long title, dense specifications, and overloaded “About this item” section.
