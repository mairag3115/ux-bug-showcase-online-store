\# Finding 02



\## Title

Product title is too long and reduces scanability



\## Type

UX Finding



\## Severity

Medium



\## Priority

Medium



\## Screen Analyzed

Amazon product detail page



\## Observation

The product title is extremely long and includes many attributes in a single line of text, such as audience, features, screen size, functions, compatibility, and resistance level.



\## Why It Matters

Users often rely on the product title to quickly understand what the item is and decide whether to continue exploring. When the title becomes too long, it is harder to scan and process essential information efficiently.



\## User Impact

\- Slower product understanding

\- Reduced scanability

\- Higher cognitive effort

\- Less clarity in first impression



\## Evidence

Use screenshot evidence showing the oversized product title on the Amazon product detail page.
