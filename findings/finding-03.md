\# Finding 03



\## Title

Key product benefits are not easy to scan quickly



\## Type

UX Finding



\## Severity

Medium



\## Priority

Medium



\## Screen Analyzed

Amazon product detail page



\## Observation

The “About this item” section includes multiple long bullet points and dense descriptive content. While the information is useful, the presentation makes it harder for users to quickly extract the most relevant benefits.



\## Why It Matters

Users often want to understand the top reasons to buy a product within a few seconds. If the key benefits are buried inside long text blocks, users may miss valuable information or lose interest.



\## User Impact

\- Slower benefit recognition

\- More effort required to understand product value

\- Reduced clarity in purchase decision-making

\- Higher cognitive load during product review



\## Evidence

Use screenshot evidence showing the “About this item” section with long descriptive bullet points.
