\# Project Overview



\## Project Title

UX + Bug Showcase – Amazon Store Experience



\## Analysis Type

Visual QA + UX Review



\## Main Goal

To analyze the user experience of Amazon as an online store and identify friction points, usability issues, and possible bugs through a visual and structured review.



\## Target User

A person browsing an online store to search for products, compare options, review product details, and make a purchase decision.



\## Core User Flow

1\. Open the online store

2\. Search for a product

3\. Review search results

4\. Open a product detail page

5\. Evaluate product information

6\. Identify possible pain points in the shopping experience



\## Areas Evaluated

\- Homepage navigation

\- Search experience

\- Product listings

\- Product detail clarity

\- Visual hierarchy

\- User friction points

\- General usability



\## Expected Outcome

Create a visual showcase of findings that demonstrates QA thinking, UX awareness, and the ability to identify opportunities for product improvement.
